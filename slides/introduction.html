Minimal Computing: what and why

Who will be able to see your work in 10 years?

Will they be able to see it or use it?

Minmal doesn't necessarily mean "simple"

Minimal could mean:

  • Constrained
  • Underfunded
  • Fewer total number of technologies
  • Easier to maintain long-term

Fewer "dependencies"

Formats that are "future proofed"

Open tools can be free as in kitten not free as in beer

Higher likelihood that it will keep working into the future because of simpler component parts with fewer dependencies

Wax:

A minimal computing publishing tool

What is Wax?

  • Leveraged Jekyll and Github Pages
  • Uses IIIF (International Image Interoperability Framework
  • Uses OpenSeaDragon viewer but can use any other
  • Creates a flexible simple site with a robust image viewer
